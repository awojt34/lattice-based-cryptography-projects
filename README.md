# Lattice-Based Cryptography — Projects

SageMath implementations and analytical reports from the 
Cryptology and Cybersecurity programme at the Military 
University of Technology (WAT), Warsaw.  
Reports are written in Polish.

---

- **Gauss-Lagrange Algorithm** — foundational lattice theory: 
  basis representation, lattice geometry and two-dimensional 
  basis reduction via the Gauss-Lagrange algorithm.

- **NTRU Cryptosystem** — implementation and analysis of the 
  NTRU public-key encryption scheme, including key generation, 
  encryption, decryption and correctness testing.
